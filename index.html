About Me

I'm an incoming Ph.D. student in the Paul G. Allen School of Computer Science & Engineering at the University of Washington, advised by Jerry Li. I received my B.S. from the Turing Class at Peking University. View my CV (July 2025).

My primary research interests lie at the intersection of TCS & math with diverse topics including high-dimensional probability, quantum, learning theory, etc. I'm also interested in understanding mysterious phenomena in deep learning.

During my undergraduate studies, I was fortunate to learn from great people across various fields to understand my interests more clearly. I was advised by Shaofeng Jiang on streaming geometric algorithms, Tongyang Li on quantum algorithms, and Zhiyuan Li on deep learning theory.

If you want to chat, feel free to reach out at yuchen21@stu.pku.edu.cn!
