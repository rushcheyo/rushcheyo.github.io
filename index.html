About Me

I'm a third-year CS undergrad at Peking University and a member of the Turing Class.

I'm broadly interested in understanding modern deep learning methods through a combination of theoretical and empirical analysis, with a current focus on optimization.

During my undergraduate studies, I worked on theoretical computer science (TCS) and was fortunate to be advised by Shaofeng Jiang, Tongyang Li, Kuan Cheng, and Tianren Liu, among others. I'm also grateful for the guidance from Zhiyuan Li, Kaifeng Lyu, and Lei Wu in deep learning theory.

Currently, I'm visiting the Simons Institute at UC Berkeley.

If you want to chat, feel free to reach out at yuchen21@stu.pku.edu.cn!
